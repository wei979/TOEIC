Question 1.  
 
Never ______________ such a perfectly restored Roman-era residence in all his years working as an archaeologist.
(A) had Sir Evans seen(正解)
(B) saw Sir Evans
(C) was Sir Evans to be seen
(D) Sir Evans will see
